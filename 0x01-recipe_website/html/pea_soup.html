Black-Eyed Pea Soup

A fantastic New Year's supper is this! Ideal for a chilly winter night. Wonderful comfort meal accompany with cornbread.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound bulk pork sausage
  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 large onion, diced
  • 4 cups water
  • 3 (15 ounce) cans black-eyed peas, drained
  • 1 (28 ounce) can diced tomatoes
  • 1 (10 ounce) can diced tomatoes with green chile peppers, undrained
  • 1 teaspoon Worcestershire sauce
  • ¾ teaspoon garlic salt
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • 1 (4 ounce) can chopped green chilies
  • 4 teaspoons molasses
  • 4 beef bouillon cubes
  • ¼ teaspoon ground black pepper
  • ¼ teaspoon ground cumin

Method of Cook

  1. Cook and mix the ground beef, pork sausage, and onion in a sizable Dutch oven over medium heat for 10 to 12 minutes, or until the meat is no longer pink. Drain any extra grease. Pour the water in and then toss in the other ingredients until well combined. These include the black-eyed peas, diced tomatoes, tomatoes with green chiles, Worcestershire sauce, garlic salt, salt, canned green chilies, molasses, beef bouillon cubes, black pepper, and cumin.

  2. The soup should be brought to a boil, then simmered for 45 minutes with the lid on.
